01.03.2026

Theft or Inspiration with Templates
In web development or when using HTML/CSS templates, the question often arises: Where does inspiration end and theft begin?

Templates offer an excellent way to understand layouts, structures, and design ideas, and to implement your own projects more quickly. They serve as learning material when you study the approaches of other developers.

Key points:
  • Respect license terms: Many templates allow modifications, private use, or commercial use only under certain conditions.
  • Inspiration vs. copying: Conscious adaptation of ideas is allowed, exact copying without permission is not.
  • Encourage creativity: Use templates to develop your own designs rather than just taking content.
Proper use of templates supports creative learning and the development of original, legally safe projects.
