1.0 Advertisement: Discover the eLibrary

The eLibrary is a German-language digital library for works of literature, multimedia, etc.

What is the purpose of the project? The project is intended to provide support for everyday school life and schooling.

Why is the project useful? School is difficult. In order to make school life as smooth as possible, people usually resort to aids. In this case, it is the eLibrary, which contains many things: a digital school assistant designed for the BÃ¼ndner Kantonsschule Chur (BKS Assistant), a simple way to practise mental arithmetic (Kopfrechner) and a whole host of reference works in linguistics, as well as huge summaries for subjects relevant to the Matura examination (WfG series and Co.) .

After five months of development, the eLibrary was released on 3 May 2025 via GitHub. The next updates followed a few months later (August 2025, April 2026) and further updates will follow.
